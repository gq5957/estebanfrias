Initiative velocity vs. system velocity

AI · Systems · Governance

Initiative velocity measures how quickly new work is introduced.

System velocity measures how efficiently the organization can absorb, coordinate, and complete that work.

Without redesign, added activity compounds friction.

Governance without architecture becomes theater.
