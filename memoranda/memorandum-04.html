Why transformation programs decay

AI · Systems · Governance

Most transformation programs do not fail abruptly. They decay.

Dual systems emerge: the formal program and the informal operating structure.

Behavior adapts to structure. If structure remains constant, behavior reverts.

Governance without architecture becomes theater.
